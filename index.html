This project is the final project of the course COMP7502 of HKU, 2016-2017 Summer, developed by Henry Pan and based on the previous work of Color-Thief and ColorTunes. Dominant color and color palette generation is based on the MMCQ (Median Cut Color Quantization) Algorithm. Licensed under the MIT license.
